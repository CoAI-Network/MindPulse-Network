# Community Guidelines — CoAI Network

1. **Respect** — Treat everyone with empathy and professionalism.
2. **Source your claims** — Provide references where possible.
3. **Privacy** — No personal data without explicit consent.
4. **No harm** — No content enabling real-world harm (weapons, malware, unregulated experimentation).
5. **Transparency** — Disclose affiliations and potential conflicts of interest.
6. **Accessibility** — Avoid unexplained jargon in public discussions.
7. **Moderation** — Decisions are visible and appealable.
8. **Ethical Projects** — Projects involving human experimentation must go through internal ethics review.
