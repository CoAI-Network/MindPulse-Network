## üîπ General Questions

### What is CoAI Network?
CoAI Network is a global initiative that connects developers, researchers, companies, ethicists, artists, and citizens to build **responsible, human-centered AI**.  
It is both a technical and cultural community, blending innovation with ethics, creativity, and inclusivity.

### Who can join?
Anyone interested in artificial intelligence, ethics, or collaborative innovation is welcome.  
We value diversity of backgrounds ‚Äî from technical experts to curious citizens, from artists to entrepreneurs.

### Is CoAI Network free to join?
Yes. Participation in GitHub, Discord, and community events is free of charge.  
Some external tools or platforms may have their own costs, but CoAI Network itself is open and accessible.

## üîπ Participation

### How can I contribute?
- Join discussions on GitHub and Discord.  
- Share projects in the `/projects` folder or *Show & Tell* threads.  
- Suggest improvements to documentation or guidelines.  
- Participate in events, debates, and interviews.  

### Do I need technical expertise?
No. Contributions can be technical, ethical, artistic, or cultural.  
We encourage interdisciplinary collaboration and welcome non-technical perspectives.

### How are decisions made?
Decisions are guided by the [Mission](./mission.md), [Values](./values.md), and [Code of Ethics](./code-of-ethics.md).  
Community input is collected through Discussions, and moderators ensure alignment with principles.

## üîπ Ethics and Guidelines

### What ethical principles guide the community?
Our [Code of Ethics](./code-of-ethics.md) emphasizes **human dignity, inclusivity, transparency, collaboration, and sustainability**.  
All members are expected to uphold these principles in their contributions.

### What happens if someone violates the guidelines?
Moderators may issue warnings, suspend participation, or remove members if necessary.  
We prioritize dialogue and constructive resolution, but community safety comes first.

## üîπ Privacy and Data

### What information does CoAI Network collect?
We collect minimal data: usernames, contributions, and basic activity logs.  
Sensitive personal information is not required or stored.  
See our [Privacy Policy](./privacy-policy.md) for details.

### Can I request deletion of my data?
Yes. Members may request removal of their data at any time.  
We respect the right to privacy and control over personal information.

### Are there community events?
Yes. We host talks, debates, and interviews to highlight diverse voices and explore ethical challenges in AI.  
Announcements are shared in GitHub Discussions and Discord.
